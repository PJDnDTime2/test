Player Queue

Register to play in this Friday's episode of Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime. Signups are open between Friday and Wednesday. The chosen players no a given week are announced on Thursday on discord. The first game each week has special priority for new players, so don't be afraid to sign up if you've never played with us before! The second and third games follow our normal player selection process - A player's priority to play is based on their points. Points are earned by watching our stream. Every time a player participates in Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime, their point total is halved. If you sign up, but don't get into a game, you'll recieve 10 consolation points to help you get in next time.

Sign up each week that you wish to be considered to play. The queue resets every week after that week's players are announced!

Tier Preference

Characters in Pete & Jeremy's DnDTime have an adventuring tier based on their level. Please select the tiers that you are interested in playing and for which you have a character of the appropriate level. Do not sign up for a tier for which you do not have an eligible character.
See the Playing in DnDTime to learn more about character tiers.

Games are expected to run approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes. Please be on our Discord server and responsive at least 15 minutes prior to the scheduled start time of your game.
