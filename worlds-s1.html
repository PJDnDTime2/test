DnDTime Worlds - Season 1

Season 1 of DnDTime Worlds was all about buildling the world of Empyrion! The Empyrion project was something of a 'twitch makes a D&D setting' experiment, with everything ranging from locations and NPCs to the gods themselves was created live on the air! While everything was built for Empyion, there are some definite gems created for Empyrion that can easily be used in any setting including your own homebrew games!

Check out the Season 1 'living document' for a scratchpad of notes about Empyrion!
