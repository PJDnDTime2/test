Waterdeep: Dragon Heist

Waterdeep: Dragon Heist is a short campaign based on the adventure module from Wizards of the Coast of the same name. Our cast for this campaign included Farful13, Cardwatch, Dangerou5Bacon, and DnDTime Pete as players and DnDTime Jeremy as the Dungeon Master!

This grand urban caper pits our heroes’ skill and bravado against villains the likes of which they’ve never faced before. Let the dragon hunt begin!
