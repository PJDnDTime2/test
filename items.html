Item Submissions

We're always looking for fresh and exciting items for Bartholomew's Big Bag Bonanza! Players who wish to create their own magic items for consideration can submit them here, and if an item that you create is pulled from the bag on-stream, we'll give you a shout-out for your contribution. A one-time 500 Bartholomew Buck 'Thank You' is awarded to players for their first submission officially accepted into the bag. Note that we have quite the backlog of submitted items, so this 'Thank You' might come a considerable amount of time after your initial submission.

     Items submitted for consideration should generally follow our item deisgn guidelines. You can break the mold from time to time, however if you choose to do so, be diligent with your design. Any item which would require substantial revision on our end to make it appropraite or playable in DnDTime will not be considered.

     With that all in mind, go wild and create some amazing magic items!
